Welcome to Travel Buddy!

Every boarding pass has a flight number on it. To find your flight number, look at your boarding pass or digital boarding pass. The flight number will be listed as "Flight" and will begin with 2-3 letters followed by numbers. If you don't have a flight number and want to try our app, use our example boarding pass shown here!

Powered by Open Weather

Open Weather is a trusted source of weather data. Our app receives the most up-to-date forecast so that you’ll know if you need to pack that umbrella in your carry-on.

Powered by Google Maps

Google Maps API is our app’s co-pilot, providing us with accurate coordinates to your destination so that we can accurately predict the weather for you.
