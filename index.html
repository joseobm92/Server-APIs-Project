Welcome to Travel Buddy!

Every boarding pass has a flight number on it. To find your flight number, look at your boarding pass or digital boarding pass. The flight number will be listed as "Flight" and will begin with 2-3 letters followed by numbers. If you don't have a flight number and want to try our app, use our example boarding pass shown here!
